PowderPal

PowderPal is a comprehensive snow reporting application designed to provide detailed insights into snow conditions at resorts worldwide. Users can access real-time data, such as snowfall amounts, base depths, and weather forecasts.

May 2023
Fullstack Developer

Design Philosophy

The creation of PowderPal revolves around simplicity and utility. We aim to create an intuitive horizontal comparison tool for snow resorts, prioritizing ease of use and accessibility, helping skiers and snowboarders make decisions to achieve the highest satisfaction on their visits. Through thoughtful interface design and comprehensive data presentation, we empower users to make informed decisions effortlessly.
